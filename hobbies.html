Dowhill mountain Bikes

Downhill discipline is designed for the advanced and extreme riders whose love for the speed brings them blazing down the roughest single-track descenrs through rock gardens, massive drops and gnarly root sections. Downhill races are very intense and exciting to watch, with one rider racing down a defined downhill section, at any one time, in the fastest time possible.

Downhill riders will need an good sense of balance to navigate through the toughest terrains under high speeds.
Downhill bikes usually have a slacker geometry, longer rear and front travels and the bigger rotors in the disc brakes for the added stability and the control which the discipline demands.The front travel for the downhill bike is typically 200mm or 209mm depending on which manufacturer you obtained them from.The bikes also sport fewer/smaller gears for added clearance over big obstacles.They are rugged and much heavier which makes them unsuited for uphill climbs.
Full-face helmets are a must coupled with goggles to block out rock deflection
